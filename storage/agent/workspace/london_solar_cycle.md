## Twilight Analysis

| Solstice | Astronomical Twilight |
|----------|----------------------|
| Summer | **None** — sun never drops below -18° |
| Winter | Full cycle — 5:59 to 17:57 |

At London's 51.5°N latitude, summer nights never achieve true darkness.
The sun dips only to about -15° at its lowest, remaining in nautical
twilight all night. This is why London summer evenings feel "endless."
