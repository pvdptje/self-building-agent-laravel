## Correlation Observations

- **No correlation with GDP**: Germany (richest) and Brazil (middle-income) both have more holidays than France (richer than Japan, fewer holidays)
- **May is universally dense**: all 4 countries peak in May (Easter cycle + Labour Day)
- **Germany's 8 regional holidays** reflect its federal structure — unique among the four
- **Brazil's Carnival** is 2 days but classified as Bank/Optional, not Public
- **France has the fewest** (11) despite strong labor protections — holidays ≠ labor rights
