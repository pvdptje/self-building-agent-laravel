## Analysis

Despite vastly different caloric density (539 vs 42 kcal/100g), both products
score Nutri-Score E — the lowest grade. Different reasons:
- **Nutella**: very high fat (30.9g) + very high sugar (56.3g)
- **Coca-Cola**: moderate sugar (10.6g) but zero nutritional value (0 protein, 0 fiber)

Both are NOVA 4 (ultra-processed). Nutella provides 6.3g protein from hazelnuts
and milk; Coca-Cola provides none.
