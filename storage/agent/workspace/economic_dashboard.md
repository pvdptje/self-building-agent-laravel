## Analysis

- **France**: high-income, service-based, 83yr life expectancy
- **India**: lower-middle-income, rapidly growing, 72yr life expectancy, 21× the population

Japan and France are comparable (0.73× GDP ratio) but Japan supports 1.8× the population
with marginally higher life expectancy (84 vs 83 years).

Brazil sits at the middle-income inflection point — $10,713 GDP with 76yr life expectancy
and a 212M population that's larger than France + Japan combined.
